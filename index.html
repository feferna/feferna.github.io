Short Bio

Currently, I am a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical Engineering at Oklahoma State University. I am researching the automatic design of deep neural networks using evolutionary computation under the supervison of Dr. Gary G. Yen. I have a Masters degree in Electrical Engineering from Campinas State University in Brazil and a Associate degree in Industrial Mechatronics from the Federal Institute of Technology of Ceara, also in Brazil.

Research Interests

My current research interests include computer vision, machine learning, deep learning, and evolutionary computation.
